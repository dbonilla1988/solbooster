Frequently Asked Questions

What is SolBoost Volume Bot?

SolBoost Volume Bot is a service that helps boost the transaction volume for your Solana token by executing buy and sell transactions. This leads to increased visibility and creates market traction, making it ideal for new projects that need more market presence.

How does it work?

Once you select a service package and provide your token's Contract Address (CA), the bot begins executing transactions based on the package’s configuration. This helps create FOMO (Fear of Missing Out), drives organic market trends, and makes your token more appealing to potential investors by increasing both volume and visibility.

Why is volume important for my project?

Volume is crucial for gaining visibility. Higher transaction volume often leads to a higher ranking on market lists, which increases the likelihood of investors noticing your token. High volume also signals liquidity and trust, encouraging other buyers to invest in your project.

How do I choose the right package for my project?

We offer several packages to fit different needs. The Basic Package is ideal for newly launched or small projects aiming for a simple boost. The Advanced and Premium Packages are suited for projects that need consistent activity and want to attract a larger audience. The Customizable Package lets you fine-tune the buy/sell ratios to match your specific goals.

How do I use the SolBoost Volume Bot?

You can use our Telegram bot to easily select and activate a package. Simply type /start to begin, select your desired service, and follow the prompts to provide the token contract address and complete the payment.

How do I know if my payment was successful?

Once you send the required amount of SOL to the wallet address provided by the bot, our system will verify the transaction. You will receive a confirmation message once the payment is verified, and the volume boosting will begin.
